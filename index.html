Andrea Porelli

Data Scientist

I graduated from the Master of Science in Computational Science and Engineering program at the J. Paulson School of Engineering at Harvard University. After completing my master’s, I worked as Data Scientist consultant building predictive models to solve business challenges. I am ready to join a tech company where I could apply my expertise.

I hold a Ph.D. in Urban Regeneration and Economic Development from the Department of Architecture and Planning at the Università Mediterranea di Reggio Calabria. My research integrates urban planning with public policy applying models for inclusive urban economic development (link to abstract).

I have been a visiting scholar at the Department of Economics at Northeastern University, Boston, as part of a research group developing a multidisciplinary approach for mapping local economic development - and industrial agglomeration - using Geographic Information System (link to materials).

I have worked as Data Scientist and Urban Planner consultant for local and international firms building predictive models to inform investments startegies and to evaluate development program. I have also been Teaching Fellow for the course CS209A and AC295 in Data Science helping in the development of the supporting materials (link to website).
