Andrea Porelli

I graduated from the Master of Science in Computational Science and Engineering program at the J. Paulson School of Engineering at Harvard University. I joined the program with the goal of learning Data Science and Machine Learning techniques to inform Urban Policy using Big Data.

I hold a Ph.D. in Urban Regeneration and Economic Development from the Department of Architecture and Planning at the Università Mediterranea di Reggio Calabria. My research integrates urban planning with public policy applying models for inclusive urban economic development.

I have been a visiting scholar at the Department of Economics at Northeastern University to develop a multidisciplinary approach for mapping local economic development. [LINK] and features extraction

I am also Urban Planner and Data Scientist Consultant and I work with local and international firms on predictive models to inform investments startegies and to evaluate development program. I am a licensed Architect in Italy and I hold a Master’s degree in Architecture from the Universita’ degli studi di Ferrara.
